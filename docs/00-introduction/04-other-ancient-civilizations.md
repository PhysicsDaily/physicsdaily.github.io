# Other Ancient Civilizations

While India looked inward to the nature of matter and mind, other civilizations were mastering the macroscopic world.

*   **Mesopotamia & Egypt:** Masters of **observation**. They mapped the stars with precision to predict floods and eclipses, laying the groundwork for timekeeping and astronomy. Their engineering feats (pyramids) displayed an intuitive mastery of mechanics and statics.
*   **Ancient China:** Masters of **invention**.
    *   **Compass:** They discovered magnetism and harnessed it for navigation.
    *   **Gunpowder:** An early application of chemical potential energy.
    *   **Seismoscope:** Zhang Heng invented the first device to detect earthquakes, demonstrating an early understanding of wave propagation.
