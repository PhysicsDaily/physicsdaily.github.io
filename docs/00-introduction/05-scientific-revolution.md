# The Scientific Revolution to Modern Era

The torch of knowledge passed from the East to the Islamic Golden Age, and eventually to Renaissance Europe, sparking the revolution that gave us the physics we use today.

*   **Galileo:** Introduced the telescope and the concept of **experimentation** over pure logic.
*   **Newton:** Unified the heavens and earth with **Gravity**.
*   **Maxwell:** Unified electricity and magnetism into **Electromagnetism**.
*   **Einstein:** Shattered our notions of absolute time and space with **Relativity**.
*   **Planck, Bohr, Heisenberg:** Revealed the strange, probabilistic world of the **Quantum**.

Today, we stand on the shoulders of these giants—from the Vedic Rishis to the Quantum Physicists—looking out into a universe that is more mysterious and beautiful than they could have ever imagined.
